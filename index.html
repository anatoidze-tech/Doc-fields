Subject of the Agreement

In accordance with the present Agreement and legislation of Georgia, the Contractor is appointed to the position of Chief Marketing Officer of Merkeleon Georgia LLC and accordingly, the Contractor should perform the labour work in accordance with this Agreement.

Contract duration

This Contract is valid for 1 (one) year. The labor relation is deemed terminated by expiration of the duration of the contract. The termination of the contract by expiration does not require any additional notification. The Employee shall be subject to probation period commencing from the starting date for 3 (three) months period.

Obligations of the Parties

Contractor shall perform his/her duties personally and in a conscientious, diligent and qualified manner; take care of the Employer's property, entrusted equipment and inventory and keep it safe and undamaged during the whole term of employment.

Compensation

The employer shall pay the Contractor a monthly salary as agreed upon in writing. Payments shall be made on the last business day of each month via bank transfer to the account designated by the Contractor.

Confidentiality

The Contractor agrees to keep all proprietary information, trade secrets, and business strategies of the Company strictly confidential during and after the term of this Agreement. This obligation shall survive termination of the Agreement.

Non-Disclosure

Neither party shall disclose the terms of this Agreement to any third party without prior written consent from the other party. Any breach of this provision shall constitute material breach of the Agreement.

Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Georgia. Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved through arbitration in Tbilisi.

Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions, representations, or agreements relating to the subject matter herein.
